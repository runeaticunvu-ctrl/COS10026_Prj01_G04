📚 Front-End Web Developer 📚

📚 Front-End Web Developer 📚

Reference Number: #DE32J7

Salary: $80,000 AUD

Location: Melbourne, VIC (Hybrid)

Schedule: Full-time, Mon-Fri (Flexible hours)

What You'll be Doing

  • Craft visually stunning, user-first interfaces that bring learning to life.
  • Transform bold ideas into seamless digital experiences that empower learners everywhere.
  • Champion performance and accessibility to ensure no learner is left behind.
  • Continuously innovate and elevate our platform through clean, scalable code.

What you Bring

Essential Requirements

  • A passion for coding that goes beyond syntax—its about impact.
  • Strong command of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with modern frameworks like React or Vue.
  • A commitment to accessibility and inclusive design practices.

Preferable Requirements

  • A curiosity for the future of digital education and edutainment.
  • An eye for design and an appreciation for great user experiences.
  • Familiarity with Git and collaborative development workflows.
  • Understanding of APIs and how front-end connects to the bigger picture.

What's in it for you?

  • Be part of a mission thats reshaping how the world learns.
  • Flexible work options that fit your lifestyle, not the other way around.
  • Opportunities to grow, upskill, and unlock your full potential.
  • A culture that celebrates creativity, collaboration, and fresh ideas.

Reporting Line

You will report to the Lead Front-End Developer, working within a dynamic, cross-functional team of innovators, educators, and creators. Together, you will align on a shared vision: delivering impactful, accessible learning experiences that make a real difference.

How to Apply

  1. Submit your resume and portfolio through our careers portal.
  2. Tell us your story—what drives your passion for tech and learning.
  3. Complete a short technical challenge to showcase your skills.
  4. Meet our team and explore how you can make an impact with us.

UX/UI Designer

✨ UX/UI Designer ✨

Reference Number: #FG67V2

Salary: $75,000 AUD

Location: Melbourne, VIC (On-site / Hybrid options available)

Schedule: Full-time, Mon-Fri (Flexible start/finish times)

What You'll be Doing

  • Design meaningful, intuitive experiences that turn learning into a journey.
  • Put users at the heart of every decision through research and empathy.
  • Bring ideas to life with wireframes, prototypes, and polished designs.
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure every detail lands perfectly.

What you Bring

Essential Requirements

  • A portfolio that tells the story of thoughtful, user-centered design
  • Expertise in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch
  • A deep understanding of UX principles and human-centered design
  • A drive to create inclusive, accessible digital experiences

Preferable Requirements

  • A passion for edutainment and engaging digital storytelling
  • Basic front-end knowledge to bridge design and development
  • Experience with usability testing and iterative design
  • Familiarity with design systems and scalable components

What's in it for you?

  • Shape the future of learning through creativity and innovation
  • Work in an environment where your ideas genuinely matter
  • Enjoy flexibility, balance, and a people-first culture
  • Access tools, support, and opportunities to level up your craft

Reporting Line

You will report to the Head of Product Design, collaborating with a diverse team of developers, strategists, and educators. Your work will directly influence product direction, ensuring every experience is as impactful as it is intuitive.

How to Apply

  1. Share your resume and portfolio showcasing your best work
  2. Tell us what great design means to you

  4. Join us for a conversation about your vision and our mission
